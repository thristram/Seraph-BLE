Detailed Description

Zero or more of these events may be raised after a call to GattDiscoverAllPrimaryServices() and before the GATT_DISC_ALL_PRIM_SERV_CFM event, or, after a call to GattFindIncludedServices() and before the GATT_FIND_INCLUDED_SERV_CFM event.
